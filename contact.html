Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take? +

Domestic shipping within Egypt typically takes 3-5 business days. International shipping times vary by destination but generally range from 7-14 business days. Express shipping options are available for an additional fee.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Yes, we ship worldwide to over 50 countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. You can calculate shipping costs during checkout.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in original condition. Custom-made or personalized items may not be eligible for return unless there is a manufacturing defect. Please contact our customer service team for return instructions.

Are your products authentic Egyptian crafts? +

Absolutely! All our products are authentic Egyptian crafts made by skilled artisans using traditional techniques. We work directly with artisans across Egypt to ensure the authenticity and quality of every piece.

Do you offer custom orders? +

Yes, we specialize in custom orders! Whether you're looking for personalized jewelry, custom artwork, or unique home decor, our artisans can create something special just for you. Contact us with your ideas, and we'll work with you to bring your vision to life.
